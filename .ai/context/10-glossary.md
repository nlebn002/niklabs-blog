# Glossary

- Post: published blog content visible to readers.
- Draft: unpublished or in-progress post content.
- Slug: stable URL identifier for a post.
- Tag: classification label attached to posts.
- Media asset: uploaded file stored through the media/object-storage flow.
- Module: backend business area with its own boundaries, services, and persistence ownership.
- Spec: per-feature brief in `.ai/specs/` used as a handoff contract.
